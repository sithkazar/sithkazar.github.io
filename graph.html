Notes:
  • Some of the numbers are estimated based on the given rank. (For example, we are told that Chen Lin Jian is a 5 Star Bronze. Since we do not know the exact amount I just put him at the start of the rank).
  • I made some assumptions on what level of soul force is needed for the higher ranks. These are just guess and will need to be corrected as we have more information. Given what little we have been told, I have made the graph rise in a logrithmic scale.
  • I have added Wei Nan, Zhu Xiangjun and Zhang Ming to the graphs under "Three Minions." There didn't seem to be a reason to add them separately as we always get information about them together and it is always the same. Though we are not told what their starting Soul Force is I made a guess at about ten. That seemed about correct as Nie Li and Lu Piao were also red soul realms and were around that.
